Registering Your Restaurant on Fooodis.com

Get Started with Your Restaurant’s Online Presence

Registering your restaurant on Fooodis.com is the first step toward managing your operations digitally. In this section, we’ll guide you through finding the registration link on the homepage, filling out essential details like your restaurant’s name and owner information, and submitting your registration form. Once completed, you’ll receive an email with your login credentials, including an automatically generated password. With this information, you can access your dashboard and start customizing your restaurant’s digital experience.

Registrera din restaurang på Fooodis.com

Steg-för-steg-guide för att börja din registrering:
1. Gå till registreringssidan
○ På Fooodis.com:s startsida, hitta länken "Registrera" i menyn längst upp till höger på skärmen (1). Klicka på denna länk för att komma till registreringssidan.

2. Ange din restaurangs information
  ○ På sidan Registrera din restaurang (2), fyll i de obligatoriska fälten:
    ○ Restaurangnamn: Ange din restaurangs namn.
    ○ Ägarens namn: Ange ditt fullständiga namn.
    ○ Ägarens e-postadress: Fyll i din e-postadress.
    ○ Ägarens telefonnummer: Skriv in ditt kontakttelefonnummer.

3. Spara informationen
  ○ När du har fyllt i uppgifterna klickar du på knappen "Spara" längst ner i registreringsformuläret (3).
  ○ Du kommer att få ett e-postmeddelande med inloggningsuppgifterna för din restaurang, inklusive ett automatiskt genererat lösenord (observera: lösenordet är inte skiftlägeskänsligt).

Accessing Your Restaurant Dashboard

Log in and Manage Your Restaurant’s Daily Operations

After completing your registration, you’ll have access to Fooodis.com’s powerful dashboard where you can manage your restaurant’s menus, orders, and more. This part of the guide explains how to log in to your account using the credentials sent to you via email. We’ll also cover how to access the login page anytime from the homepage, ensuring you can stay connected to your restaurant’s operations. Once logged in, you’ll be able to make key adjustments and improvements to enhance your restaurant’s performance.

Accessing Your Restaurant Dashboard

How to Log in and Manage Your Restaurant:

4. Access Your Dashboard
  ○ Once your account is created, you can log in to the Fooodis platform to access your restaurant’s dashboard (4). Here, you can manage your menu, orders, and other operational settings.

5. Login Anytime via the Homepage
  ○ To log in at any time, click the "Login" button on the homepage of Fooodis.com (5). This will take you to the login page.

6. Use Your Login Credentials
  ○ On the login page (6), enter your username (the email address you registered with) and the password you received via email. Click Sign In to access your dashboard.

Recovering Your Password and Choosing a Plan

Stay Secure and Select the Perfect Plan for Your Restaurant

In the event that you forget your password, Fooodis.com makes it easy to recover your account. This part of the guide shows you how to reset your password by entering your registered email address and receiving a recovery link. Additionally, once logged in, you can explore various subscription plans that suit the size and scope of your restaurant. Whether you’re managing a small bistro or a large establishment, Fooodis offers flexible plans that cater to your specific needs. Simply head to the “Pricing Plans” section in your dashboard to get started.

Recovering Your Password and Choosing a Plan

How to Recover Your Password and Select a Plan:

7. Forgot Your Password?
  ○ If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password" link located under the login form (7).

8. Recover Your Password
  ○ On the password recovery page (8), enter the email address you used to register your restaurant. You will receive an email with a link to reset your password.

9. Choose a Plan for Your Restaurant
  ○ Once you’ve registered, you can select a subscription plan based on your restaurant’s size and needs. Fooodis offers various plans, from small restaurant packages to options for larger establishments.
  ○ On your dashboard, click "Pricing Plans" to view and select the plan that best fits your restaurant.

Common Questions About Registering Your Restaurant on Fooodis.com

Starting your journey with Fooodis.com is easy, but we know you might have a few questions when registering your restaurant. To make things smoother, we’ve put together a list of frequently asked questions (FAQs) that cover the entire registration process. From filling out the registration form to receiving your login credentials and resetting your password, these FAQs address the most common queries to help you get your restaurant set up quickly and efficiently.

How do I register my restaurant on Fooodis.com?
  • To register your restaurant on Fooodis.com, go to the homepage and click the "Register" link at the top of the page. This will take you to the registration form where you’ll enter your restaurant name, owner information, and contact details. Once you submit the form, you’ll receive an email with your login credentials. These will allow you to access your dashboard and start managing your restaurant online.
What information do I need to register my restaurant?
  • When registering your restaurant, you’ll need to provide the following details: your restaurant’s name, the owner’s name, email address, and phone number. This information ensures that your account is set up correctly and that you can receive important communications, including your login credentials.
How do I access my restaurant’s dashboard after registration?
  • Once you’ve registered, you’ll receive an email with your login information, including your username and a password. You can access your restaurant’s dashboard by visiting the homepage of Fooodis.com and clicking "Login" in the top menu. Use the credentials sent to you via email to sign in and start managing your restaurant.
What if I forget my password?
  • –
  • –
    If you forget your password, you can easily reset it. On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter the email address you used when registering your restaurant. Fooodis.com will send you an email with a link to reset your password, ensuring that you can quickly regain access to your account.
How do I choose a subscription plan for my restaurant?
  • After logging into your restaurant’s dashboard, you can explore the various subscription plans offered by Fooodis.com. Each plan is designed to cater to different restaurant sizes and needs, from small eateries to large establishments. Click on the "Pricing Plans" option in your dashboard to view and select the plan that best suits your business.
Can I log in to my restaurant’s account anytime?
  • Yes! Once you’ve registered, you can log in at any time by clicking the "Login" button on the homepage of Fooodis.com. Simply use the email and password you received during registration to access your dashboard and manage your restaurant.
