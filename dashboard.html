Guide to the Fooodis Dashboard Section

1. Dashboard Section Overview
○ The dashboard is accessible from the left-hand sidebar under the Dashboard tab. It provides a comprehensive view of your restaurant's performance metrics and activities.

2. Orders (30 Days)
○ This section shows the total number of orders your restaurant has received in the last 30 days, helping you understand recent sales trends and customer activity.

3. Sales Volume (30 Days)
○ This field displays the total sales revenue generated in the past 30 days. It gives you a quick glance at how much your restaurant has earned within the specified period.

4. Expenses (30 Days)
○ Here, you can track your restaurant’s expenses over the last 30 days. This data helps you manage costs and optimize profit margins.

5. Views
○ This section shows the number of views your restaurant’s online menu or ordering platform has received, giving you an idea of your visibility to potential customers.

6. Delivery Activities
○ The Delivery Activities map visualizes your delivery operations. It displays real-time delivery tracking, helping you monitor driver locations and delivery statuses.

7. Sales Value
○ The Sales Value chart provides an overview of your sales performance over time. You can analyze sales trends across specific dates or months to measure growth and adjust strategies.

8. Total Orders
○ This performance chart shows the number of total orders placed within the selected period, allowing you to monitor how busy your restaurant has been over time.

9. Expenses by Category (30 Days)
○ This section breaks down your restaurant’s expenses into various categories, such as food costs, labor, or supplies. It helps you identify which areas require better cost management.

10. Expenses by Vendor (30 Days)
○ The Expenses by Vendor chart highlights which vendors or suppliers contribute to your restaurant’s expenses. This feature assists in evaluating supplier costs and adjusting orders accordingly.

How to Use the Dashboard Effectively

○  Keep an eye on key performance indicators like orders, sales volume, and expenses to ensure your restaurant is operating efficiently.
○ Use the Delivery Activities map to monitor your delivery fleet and ensure timely service.
○ Analyze sales and expense trends using the provided charts to make informed decisions about menu pricing, staffing, and supply management.
By regularly reviewing these metrics, you can optimize your restaurant's operations, identify areas for improvement, and ensure continued success.

FAQs for the Fooodis Dashboard

The Fooodis Dashboard is a powerful tool that provides a centralized view of your restaurant’s key performance metrics. Whether you’re tracking orders, monitoring sales, or managing expenses, the dashboard gives you all the data you need to optimize operations. Below are some frequently asked questions to help you better understand and utilize the dashboard’s features.

What is the Fooodis Dashboard used for?

The Fooodis Dashboard is designed to give restaurant owners a complete overview of their business performance, including sales, orders, expenses, and delivery activities, all in one place.

How can I track my restaurant’s sales performance?

The dashboard displays Sales Volume (30 Days) and a Sales Value chart, which provide a detailed look at how much revenue your restaurant has generated and sales trends over time.

Can I monitor my deliveries in real time?

Yes, the Delivery Activities section provides a real-time map where you can track the location of delivery drivers and monitor ongoing deliveries.

How do I check how many orders I’ve received?

The Orders (30 Days) section shows the total number of orders placed within the past 30 days, allowing you to easily track customer activity and sales volume.

How can I track my expenses?

The dashboard includes both Expenses (30 Days), which shows your overall costs, and Expenses by Category/Vendor, which provides a breakdown of expenses by supplier or category (e.g., food, labor, supplies).

What is the "Views" section for?

The Views section displays how many times your restaurant’s online menu or ordering platform has been viewed, giving you insights into customer interest and online traffic.

Can I customize the dashboard data range?

Currently, the dashboard focuses on a 30-day data range for most metrics. Future updates may allow for more customizable date ranges.

How do I interpret the Total Orders graph?

The Total Orders graph gives you a visual representation of how many orders were placed over time, allowing you to spot trends, busy periods, and growth patterns.

How can I use this data to improve my restaurant?

By reviewing metrics such as sales trends, expenses by category, and delivery performance, you can make informed decisions about staffing, inventory management, menu pricing, and more.

What should I do if the data on my dashboard looks incorrect?

If any of the data seems incorrect, try refreshing the dashboard. If the issue persists, contact Fooodis support for further assistance.
