Guide to Managing Online Ordering and Tips on Fooodis

Enabling Online Ordering and Tips

Unlock efficient online ordering and tip management for your restaurant with our quick-start guide on the Fooodis platform.

Enabling Online Ordering and Tips
Accessing Restaurant Management Settings:
Navigate to the Management Interface:
  ○ Log into your Fooodis dashboard and click on 'Restaurang' or 'Restaurant Management' from the sidebar to access various settings for your restaurant.
Enable Online Ordering:
  ○ Within the Restaurant Management section, locate the option labeled 'Disable ordering'. If there is a tick in the box, click it to disable the tick, thereby enabling online ordering for your restaurant.

Setting Up Online Ordering and Tips:
Confirm Online Ordering Activation:
  ○ Ensure that the 'Disable ordering' option is unchecked. This activation allows customers to place orders online directly from your restaurant’s digital menu.
Understanding Automatic Tip Functionality:
  ○ When online ordering is enabled, the tips feature is also automatically activated. Customers will see an option to add a tip during the checkout process in the online ordering interface.
Review and Customize Settings:
  ○ Customize the online ordering settings according to your business needs. This includes setting up delivery options such as 'Dine In', 'Takeaway', or 'Deliver' and configuring payment methods like 'Cash/Card Terminal' or 'Stripe Checkout'.
Set Default Options for Tips:
  ○ While the tip feature activates automatically with online ordering, ensure that your checkout interface clearly prompts customers for tips. This setting enhances the customer experience by providing them with the opportunity to easily reward your service.

Finalizing and Testing

Save Changes:
   ○ After adjusting your settings, click the 'Save' button at the bottom of the settings page to ensure all your new configurations are applied.
Test the Ordering Process:
  ○ Conduct a test run by placing an order through your customer interface to ensure that the ordering and tip functions work seamlessly.

By following these steps, you can efficiently manage online ordering and tips through your Fooodis platform. This guide ensures that you provide an optimized online ordering experience, enabling your customers to support your restaurant directly through their purchases and gratuities. Make sure all settings reflect your current operational and service offerings to maximize the effectiveness of your digital engagement with customers.

FAQs: Enabling and Managing Online Ordering and Tips on Fooodis

Navigate through the intricacies of online ordering and tips management on Fooodis with our detailed FAQs. Whether you're looking to enhance customer convenience or have questions about setting up these features, this section provides you with clear answers and practical tips to ensure your digital ordering system is effective and user-friendly.

How do I enable online ordering in my Fooodis dashboard?

To enable online ordering, log into your dashboard, go to 'Restaurant Management', and ensure the 'Disable ordering' checkbox is unchecked. This activates the ordering system for your customers.

Is the tipping feature automatically enabled with online ordering?

Yes, when online ordering is enabled, the tipping feature is also automatically activated, allowing customers to add tips during the checkout process.

Can I customize the tipping options available to customers?

While the tipping option is preset to be straightforward, you can suggest predefined tipping amounts or percentages based on your regional and business norms by customizing your payment settings.

Conduct a test order from the customer's perspective to ensure that the ordering process is smooth and that the tipping prompt appears correctly at checkout.
