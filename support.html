Our blog provides a range of guides, tutorials, and expert tips, categorized for easy navigation. You’ll find step-by-step instructions to help you set up and optimize different features of your Fooodis dashboard.

The AI chat assistant is located at the bottom right corner of the Fooodis website and dashboard. It’s available 24/7 to guide you through any feature and provide real-time assistance whenever you need help.

If you need more personalized support, you can fill out the Contact Form on our contact page. A member of our team will get back to you promptly to assist with your query.
