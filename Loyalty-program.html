Unlock the Power of Rewards to Enhance Customer Engagement and Drive Repeat Business
The Fooodis Loyalty Program is a dynamic, customizable tool designed to help restaurants cultivate lasting relationships with their customers by rewarding loyalty and encouraging repeat visits. This program allows restaurant owners to set up and manage a comprehensive rewards system directly through the Fooodis.com dashboard.

Here’s how it works:
  ○ Setup and Customization: Restaurant owners can easily set up the loyalty program by accessing their Fooodis dashboard. From there, they can customize the program's structure, including the types of rewards offered (e.g., discounts, free items, exclusive offers) and the criteria for earning these rewards.
  ○ Point Accumulation: Customers earn points based on their purchases. The point allocation can be tailored to different products or services, providing flexibility in how rewards are distributed.
  ○ Reward Redemption: Once customers accumulate enough points, they can redeem them for rewards. The redemption process is streamlined through the Fooodis dashboard, where customers can view their points and available rewards, enhancing the user experience.
  ○ Ongoing Management: Owners have full control over the program, with capabilities to adjust settings, track program success, and analyze customer behavior and program impact.

By integrating a loyalty program, restaurants not only enhance customer satisfaction but also increase the likelihood of repeat business, ultimately fostering a community of loyal patrons who feel valued and engaged.

Setting Up Your Fooodis Loyalty Program

A Simple Guide to Launching a Reward System That Keeps Customers Coming Back

Discover how to effortlessly set up the Fooodis Loyalty Program through your dashboard, enabling you to reward repeat customers and enhance their loyalty. Follow our straightforward steps to configure point systems and reward mechanisms that resonate with your clientele.

Activating and Configuring the Loyalty Program via the App
Accessing the Loyalty Program
  ○ Login to Fooodis Dashboard:
Begin by logging into your Fooodis dashboard using your credentials.

1. Navigate to the Loyalty Program (App Section)
  ○ From the left sidebar, locate the 'App' section. Under this section, click on the 'Loyalty Program' option.

2. Enable the Loyalty ProgramActivate the Loyalty Program
  ○ In the loyalty program configuration page that appears, check the 'Enable Loyalty Program' box at the top to activate this feature for your customers.

3. Configure the Program DetailsHeading
  ○ Add a headline for your loyalty program, such as “Earn Points with Every Purchase” to grab your customers' attention.

  ○ Provide a short description that explains the purpose of the loyalty program, e.g., “Join our loyalty program and start collecting points for exciting rewards!”.

  ○ Join Now Text 1: Encourage your customers to sign up, for example, with text like: “Unlock special offers and rewards by becoming a loyalty member today!”.

  ○ Join Now Text 2: Clarify how the points system works, such as “As a member, you'll earn points for every purchase, which can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.”
  ○ Webhook (Optional)If you have a system that communicates through webhooks, input the URL in this section. This will trigger automatic updates or notifications based on loyalty point actions.
  ○ Set Initial PointsYou can set the initial number of points that each new customer receives upon joining. A common starting value could be 5 points to incentivise sign-ups.

4. Save Your Settings
Once all the fields are filled out, click the 'Save' button to finalize the setup and activate the loyalty program for your restaurant.

Digital Product: 
  ○ A downloadable or digital reward.
Active To:
  ○ Set an expiration date for the award to create urgency and manage reward availability.
Color:
  ○ Select a color that will be associated with the reward in the user interface. This helps with visual identification of different rewards.

Initial Setup and Client Selection

1. Navigating to Give Points
○ Log into your Dashboard:
Access your Fooodis dashboard by logging in with your credentials.
○ Select 'Give Points':
From the left sidebar menu, select the ‘Give Points’ option to open the points allocation interface.

2. Selecting a Client
○ Client Field
The selected client's details will appear automatically at the top, showing their name and current points balance . Use the dropdown or search by name or ID to assign points to a different client if needed.

Transaction Details and Applying Point Rules
3. Order ID Entry
○ Input the unique identifier for the transaction in the Order ID field (marked as 3 in the screenshot). This could be the order number or an internal reference.

4. Order Value Entry
○ Input the total monetary value of the customer’s order in the Order Value field.

Guide to Redeeming Awards in Your Fooodis Loyalty Program

Creating a seamless experience for redeeming awards is essential for maintaining a successful loyalty program on Fooodis. This guide will walk you through the process of how customers can redeem their earned points for awards through your Fooodis dashboard.

Mastering Award Redemption on Fooodis

1. Accessing the Redemption Interface
   ○ Navigate to Redeem Points: Log into your Fooodis dashboard and select the 'Redeem Points' ​  option from the menu to open the award redemption interface.
  ○ Redemption Page: You will see the 'Get an Award' section as shown in the screenshot.

2. Selecting the Client
  ○ Client Selection: Use the dropdown menu under the 'Client' field to select the client who wishes to redeem their points. The dropdown list includes all registered clients who have accumulated enough points for at least one award.

3. Choosing the Award
  ○ Award Selection: Next to the 'Award' field, use the corresponding dropdown to select the specific award that the client wishes to redeem. This list will display all available awards that the client has enough points to claim.

4. Redeeming the Award
  ○ Initiate Redemption: Once the client and their chosen award have been selected, click the 'Get Award' button to proceed with the redemption.
  ○ Confirmation: A confirmation message should appear to verify that the award has been successfully redeemed, and the appropriate points will be deducted from the client’s total.

Managing After Redemption
  ○ Update Client Records: Ensure that the client’s points balance is updated immediately to reflect the redemption.
  ○ Award Availability: Adjust the availability of awards as necessary, depending on stock or offer expiry dates.

Consolidated FAQs for the Fooodis Loyalty Program

Dive into our detailed FAQ section where we cover every facet of the Fooodis Loyalty Program, providing you with step-by-step instructions and insightful tips on how to effectively set up, manage, and optimize your loyalty program. Whether you’re configuring point systems, creating enticing rewards, or handling customer redemptions, our guide ensures you have the knowledge and tools at your fingertips to enhance customer engagement and drive repeat business. Explore our extensive resources to make the most of your loyalty program, and watch your customer satisfaction and retention soar.

How do I initially set up the Fooodis Loyalty Program for my restaurant?

Begin by logging into your Fooodis dashboard. Navigate to the 'Apps' section where you'll find the option to activate the Loyalty Program. Here, you can customize settings such as reward types, point values, and eligibility criteria based on your operational needs and marketing strategy.

What are the first steps to customize the loyalty program after activation?

After activation, configure the program by setting up reward tiers, point accrual rules, and redemption processes. Customize the program interface to match your brand, and set communication preferences for engaging participants through the dashboard.

How are points awarded to customers, and what rules can I set?

Points are awarded based on purchase amounts or specific customer actions (like visiting or social media sharing). In the 'Give Points' section, set rules such as a percentage of the transaction value or static points for actions. You can define different rules for different product categories or customer behaviors.

How do I modify or adjust point values after they have been set?
  • –
  • –
    Visit the 'Point Distribution' section to adjust the point values or rules. Changes can be made to accommodate promotional periods, adjust to customer feedback, or refine the program’s effectiveness.
Can I offer different types of rewards in the loyalty program?

Yes, you can offer various rewards, including discounts, free products, exclusive content, or early access to new releases. Each reward type can be customized in the 'Awards' section, where you set the required points for redemption and the validity period.

What should I consider when setting expiration dates for rewards?

Consider your business cycle, product shelf life (if applicable), and customer buying behavior. Set expiration dates to encourage timely redemptions that drive repeat business without compromising inventory management.

How does the award redemption process work for customers?

Customers log into their accounts on the Fooodis platform, view available rewards, and choose an award to redeem based on their point balance. Staff can also assist with redemptions by accessing the 'Redeem Points' section, selecting the customer’s profile, and processing the redemption.

What are best practices for handling discrepancies during award redemptions?

Establish clear protocols for verifying transaction details and managing discrepancies. Train staff to handle adjustments and maintain transparency with customers to ensure trust and satisfaction.

How can I effectively promote the loyalty program to enhance customer engagement?
  • –
    Utilize multiple channels such as in-store displays, email marketing, social media announcements, and staff interactions to educate customers about the program benefits. Regularly update promotional materials to reflect the current rewards and program enhancements.
