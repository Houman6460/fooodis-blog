FAQ: Creating and Managing QR Codes for Your Restaurant and Tables

This FAQ section is designed to address common questions about setting up and using QR codes in your restaurant. Whether you need a QR code for your entire restaurant or individual tables, this guide will help streamline the process and improve the efficiency of your service.

How do I set up WhatsApp Ordering for my restaurant?
  1. 1. Log in to your Fooodis account. If you don't have one, create an account using the "here" link on the login page.
  2. 2. Navigate to the WhatsApp Notifications section on the left-hand menu.
  3. 3. Enter your API key to connect your restaurant's WhatsApp account. Follow the instructions to obtain your API key from the WhatsApp API Dashboard.
  4. 4. Add and manage your menu items in the "Menu" section on the left-hand menu.
  5. 5. Set up your daily menu in the "Daily Menu" section by organizing items for each day of the week.
  6. 6. Display your menu online by adding items to your restaurant's header menu in the "Menu Management" section.
What are the benefits of using WhatsApp Ordering?
  • Eco-Friendly: Reduces the need for physical menus.
  • Payment Flexibility: Accept payments via Cash on Delivery or online payment methods like PayPal, Klarna, and Apple Pay.
  • Analytics: Track orders and earnings with detailed reports.
  • Customer Engagement: Direct communication with customers, enhancing loyalty and repeat business.
Can I customize the menu for each day?

Yes, in the "Daily Menu" section, you can organize and sort menu items for each day of the week. You can also customize the day titles to match your restaurant's language.

How do payments work with WhatsApp Ordering?

You can accept payments via Cash on Delivery or through payment links. The system supports over 20 payment methods, ensuring flexibility for your customers.
