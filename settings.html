Restaurant Settings Guide: Step-by-Step Manual

Seamless Configuration for Managing Your Restaurant Information

Setting up your restaurant settings on Fooodis is straightforward with our intuitive interface. This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough to help you manage your restaurant information, menus, working hours, and location seamlessly.

Restaurang Management
Essential Details and Customization for Your Restaurant
Restaurang Management Overview:
  ○ You can enter and amend important information about your restaurant, such as its name, description, address, and contact details, in this section.

1. Access Restaurang Management.
  ○ From the list on the sidebar, select 'Restaurang' .
  ○ In the top menu, select ‘Restaurang Management’ .

2. Restaurang Name and Description:
  ○ Restaurang Name: 
     ○ Enter the name of your restaurant in the 'Restaurang Name' field.
  ○ Restaurang Description: 
     ○ Provide a brief overview of your restaurant in the 'Restaurang Description' field.
  ○ Restaurang Address and Contact:
  ○ Restaurang Address: 
     ○ Enter the street address in the 'Restaurang Address' field.
  ○ Restaurang Phone: 
     ○ Add the contact phone number in the 'Restaurang Phone' field.

3. Order Settings:
  ○ Minimum Order: 
     ○ Set a minimum order value in the 'Minimum Order' field.
  ○ Average Order Prepare Time: 
     ○ Specify the average order preparation time in minutes.
  ○ Order Separation: 
     ○ Set the time slots separated by minutes for order scheduling.

Working Hours

Restaurant Settings FAQs

Navigating the settings for your restaurant can be challenging, but we’re here to make it easier. Our Restaurant Settings FAQs are designed to provide answers to your most pressing questions about configuring your restaurant’s details. From managing working hours and menu items to updating location information and adding owner details, this guide will help you optimize your settings for seamless restaurant management. Whether you're new to Fooodis or looking to enhance your current setup, find the answers you need right here.

How do I update my restaurant's name or description?
  • To update your restaurant's name or description:
  • Go to Restaurang in the sidebar.
  • In the Restaurang Management section, edit the Restaurang Name and Restaurang Description fields.
  • Click Save to apply the changes.
Can I change the restaurant logos and cover image?
  • Yes, you can change the logos and cover image by:
  • Navigating to the Restaurang Management section.
  • Uploading new images for the logos and cover.
  • Clicking Save to confirm the changes.
How do I add or update the restaurant owner’s information?
  • To add or update the owner’s information:
  • Go to Restaurang Management.
  • Scroll to the Owner Information section and fill in the relevant fields.
  • Click Save to confirm the changes.
How can I add a new menu item?
  • –
  • –
    To add a new menu item:
    Go to Restaurang in the sidebar.
    Click on Menu Management.
    Fill in the Add Menu section with the item’s name, slug, class, and target.
    Click Add Item and then Save to confirm.
Is it possible to add icons to menu items?
  • Yes, you can add icons to menu items by:
  • Going to Menu Management.
  • Selecting an icon for each item in the Add Menu section.
  • Clicking Save to confirm.
Can I change the logo link and menu text color?
  • Absolutely! In Menu Management:
  • Update the Logo link and Menu text color fields.
  • Click Save to confirm.
Can I create links to external or internal pages?
  • Yes, you can:
  • Add links to external pages (e.g., your main website) or internal pages (e.g., loyalty, card, or user pages).
  • Choose your preferred target (open directly or in a new tab).
  • Click Save to confirm.
How do I change the working hours for specific days?
  • To change working hours:
  • Go to Restaurang in the sidebar.
  • Click on Working Hours.
  • Adjust the opening and closing times for each day.
  • Click Save to confirm.
Can I add or remove shifts for my restaurant?
  • Yes, you can:
  • Go to Working Hours.
  • Click Add new shift to create additional shifts.
  • Remove shifts by clicking the trash icon next to the shift timing.
  • Click Save to apply the changes.
How do I ensure my restaurant’s map location is accurate?
  • To ensure accuracy:
  • Go to Restaurang in the sidebar.
  • Click on Location.
  • Verify that the map location matches your restaurant's address.
  • If inaccurate, update the address in the Restaurang Management section.
  • Click Save to apply the changes.
Where can I update my restaurant’s address?
  • To update the address:
  • Go to Restaurang Management.
  • Edit the Restaurang Address field.
  • Click Save to confirm.
How do I request a clone of my restaurant's website?
  • Send us an email at info@fooodis.com with your request. Include your current restaurant website details and any specific requirements for the new branch.
Can I customize the cloned website separately from the original?
  • Yes, once the new website is cloned, you will receive separate login credentials. You can customize it independently to meet the specific needs of the new branch.
How do I reset the password for the cloned website?
  • On the login page, select "Forgot Password" and provide the email address associated with the new website. You'll receive a link to reset your password.
What plan will the cloned website be on initially?
  • The cloned website will be set to the free tier by default. Please upgrade to a suitable plan to access all features.
Can I choose the same plan as my original restaurant website?
  • Yes, you can choose the same plan or any other plan that best meets the needs of your new branch.
Can I duplicate my restaurant website multiple times for different branches?
  • Absolutely! You can request as many clones as you need for each additional branch.
How long does it take to clone my restaurant's website?
  • Once we receive your request, the cloning process typically takes 1-2 business days. We'll send you the login credentials as soon as it's ready.
Do I need to provide separate contact details for each cloned website?
  • Yes, it's best to provide separate contact details (e.g., email address) for each new branch to ensure accurate and efficient communication.
Will my cloned websites have the same branding as the original?
  • Yes, the cloned websites will initially have the same branding as the original. You can customize each branch's branding as needed.
