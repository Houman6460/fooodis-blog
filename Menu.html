How to Create a Category for Your Food Menu


Creating categories for your menu helps organize your dishes and make it easier for customers to navigate through your offerings. Here’s how you can add a category on Fooodis.com.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Access the Menu Management Section
  ○ From the dashboard, navigate to the Menu section in the left sidebar.
  ○ Click on Menu to open the menu management page.

2. Enter the Restaurang Menu Management
  ○ You will be directed to the Restaurang Menu Management interface, where you can see existing categories and items.

3. View Existing Categories
  ○ On the left-hand side, you will see the list of existing categories (such as Lunch, Vegetarian, and more). You can view or edit these categories as needed by selecting them.

4. Add a New Category
  ○ To add a new category, click on the "Add New Category" button located at the top of the screen.

 5. Name Your New Category
  ○ A pop-up window will appear. Enter the name of your new category in the Category Name field. For example, you can name it Desserts, Drinks, etc.

 6. Save the Category
  ○ Once you’ve entered the category name, click Save to add it to your menu.


By following these steps, you can easily add and manage categories for your restaurant’s menu, ensuring a more organized and user-friendly experience for your customers.

Changing Category Names in Your Menu

Renaming categories is a quick and effective way to keep your menu organized and aligned with any updates or rebranding efforts. Below are the steps to change a category name based on the interface shown.

7. Select a Category to Rename
On the left panel, you’ll see the list of existing categories. To rename one, such as "Persisk," click on it  to highlight and open it.

8. Open the Category Name Field

Adding Items to a Category
Adding new menu items under specific categories helps your customers easily navigate through your restaurant’s offerings. Here’s how to assign food items to categories on Fooodis.com.

Step-by-Step Guide:
1. Go to Menu Management
  ○ From the dashboard, click on the Menu section located on the left sidebar.
  ○ This will take you to the Restaurang Menu Management screen.

2. Select a Category
  ○ Choose the category you want to add items to by clicking on it from the list on the left. For example, click on Persisk to manage items in the "Persisk" category.

3. Add New Item by Clicking the Plus Icon
  ○ To add a new item to this category, click on the Plus (+) icon at the top right of the item list . This will open the item creation form, where you can input details for the new dish.

4. Enter the Item Management Interface
  ○ After clicking the plus icon, you will be directed to the Item Management interface, where you can input the details for the new menu item.

5. Fill in Item Information
  ○ In the Item Information section, input the essential details for the dish:
  ○ Item Name: Enter the name of the dish (e.g., Chenjeh) .
  ○ Category: Ensure the correct category is selected (e.g., Persisk).
  ○ Item Description: Write a description of the dish, including any key ingredients or preparation details.

6.Add Item Price
  ○ Enter the Item Price in the designated field .
  ○ You can also add a Discounted Price and select the VAT Percentage to ensure tax compliance.

7. Upload an Item Image
  ○ Click on Select Image to upload a picture of the dish . This is essential for giving customers a visual representation of what they are ordering.

8.Set Additional Options
  ○ Toggle the switches to enable specific options for the item:
  ○ Item Available: Turn this on to make the item available to customers.
  ○ Enable Variants: Use this option if the dish has different variants (e.g., sizes, spice levels).
  ○ Enable Quantity Management: Activate this to track the quantity available for this dish.

9. Save or Delete the Item
  ○ Once you’ve entered all the necessary information, click Save to add the item to the category .
  ○ If needed, you can also delete the item by clicking Delete.

Additional Item Management Actions
After adding or modifying a menu item, you have several quick actions available at the top right of the Item Management interface to enhance your workflow:
   ○ Back to Items: Click this button to return to the full list of menu items without saving any changes. Use this if you want to review other items or exit the current view.
   ○ Copy URL: This allows you to copy the direct link to this specific menu item, which can be shared for internal reviews or edits.
   ○ Clone It: Clicking the Clone It button creates a duplicate of the current menu item. This is especially useful if you want to create a similar dish with slight variations, such as different sizes or ingredients.

Fooodis.com Menu Management - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Managing your restaurant’s menu on Fooodis.com is designed to be intuitive and efficient. Whether you're adding new items, organizing categories, or customizing the look and feel of your menu, our platform provides the flexibility you need. In this FAQ section, we address common questions and provide step-by-step guidance on menu management, customization options, and optimizing your restaurant’s digital presence. These FAQs aim to help you navigate the platform with ease and enhance the customer experience by keeping your menu updated and well-organized.

How do I create a new category for my menu?

To create a new category:

1. Navigate to the Menu section in the left sidebar of your dashboard.
2. On the Restaurang Menu Management screen, click on the Add New Category button.
3. A pop-up will appear where you can name your new category (e.g., Desserts, Drinks).
4. Click Save to finalize and add the category to your menu.

How do I rename an existing category?

To rename an existing category:
1. From the Menu section, select the category you wish to rename from the left-hand panel.
2. The current name of the category will appear in an editable field.
3. Change the name as needed, then click Save to apply the new name.

How do I add a new item to a category?

To add a new item:
1. Go to Menu Management and select the category you want to add items to.
2. Click on the Plus (+) icon at the top right of the items list.
3. Fill out the item details, including name, description, price, and VAT.
4. Upload an image of the dish if desired.
5. Click Save to add the new item.

How do I manage item availability?

You can easily manage item availability by:
1. Going to the Item Management section for a specific dish.
2. Toggle the Item Available switch to make the dish available or unavailable to customers.
3. Ensure you click Save to apply the changes.

Can I manage item variants (e.g., sizes or spice levels)?

Yes, you can:
1. In the Item Management screen, toggle the Enable Variants option.
2. You can define different variants such as sizes, spice levels, or other options.
3. Set separate prices for each variant if needed, and click Save when finished.

How can I track the quantity of a specific menu item?

To track the quantity of items:
1. Go to the Item Management interface for the dish you want to monitor.
2. Toggle the Enable Quantity Management option.
3. This will allow you to track inventory and ensure the dish availability is updated in real-time.

What does the "Clone Item" feature do?

The Clone Item feature allows you to quickly duplicate an existing menu item:
1. In the Item Management screen, click Clone It at the top right.
2. This creates a duplicate of the dish, which you can edit for slight variations (e.g., different sizes or ingredients).
3. Save the cloned item once you've made the necessary changes.

How can I apply a discounted price to a menu item?

To set a discounted price:
1. In the Item Management interface, locate the Discounted Price field.
2. Enter the reduced price for the item, and select the applicable VAT percentage if necessary.
3. Click Save to finalize the change.

How do I delete a menu item?

To delete an item:
1. Open the Item Management interface for the dish you want to remove.
2. Click on the Delete button at the bottom of the screen.
3. Confirm the deletion to permanently remove the item from the category.

How can I review or preview my menu items?

To preview your menu items:
1. In the Menu Management section, navigate to the category where your items are listed.
2. Click on the item to view all the details, such as description, price, and image.
3. You can also copy the item URL to share or review it on the front-end site.

Is it possible to create a multilingual menu?

Yes, Fooodis.com supports multilingual menus:
1. Use the Google Translate Plugin available in the Menu Settings to offer multiple language options for your customers.

Can I assign different VAT percentages to individual items?

Yes, you can assign VAT percentages for each menu item:
1. In the Item Management section, locate the VAT field.
2. Select the appropriate VAT percentage based on local tax regulations.
3. Make sure to save your changes.
