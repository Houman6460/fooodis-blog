Frequently Asked Questions

What plans are available and how do they differ?

We offer four plans to meet different business needs:

  • Basic (2-star): Essential features for small businesses just getting started
  • Standard (3-star): Additional features for growing businesses
  • Professional (4-star): Our most popular plan with advanced features for established restaurants
  • Elite (5-star): Our premium plan with all features and priority support for high-volume restaurants

Each plan includes progressively more features, with the Professional and Elite plans offering the best value for most businesses.

What discounts are available for different billing periods?

We offer increasing discounts for longer billing periods:

  • 3-Month plans: Save 10% compared to monthly billing
  • 6-Month plans: Save 15% compared to monthly billing
  • Yearly plans: Save 20% compared to monthly billing

Additionally, all plans currently have a limited-time 50% discount promotion, plus free period bonuses: 15 days free with monthly plans, 1 month free with 3-month plans, 2 months free with 6-month plans, and 3 months free with yearly plans.

How does the savings calculator work?

Our calculator helps you see how much you can save by switching to Fooodis:

  1. Enter your current monthly costs for services like booking systems, website builders, loyalty programs, and delivery integration
  2. Select your preferred currency (USD, EUR, or SEK)
  3. Choose a billing period (longer periods offer greater savings)
  4. Select the plan that best meets your needs
  5. The calculator will instantly show your monthly savings, yearly savings, and total cost reduction percentage

The calculator also generates a special discount code that you can use during checkout to claim your 50% limited-time discount.

How do I apply the 50% discount?

To apply the 50% discount:

  1. Use our calculator to select your preferred plan and billing period
  2. Copy the unique discount code shown in the coupon card
  3. During checkout on Fooodis.com, paste your code in the coupon field
  4. The 50% discount will be automatically applied to your order

This offer is only available for a limited time, as indicated by the countdown timer.

Can I change my plan or currency later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to additional features. When downgrading, changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

You can also change your billing currency (USD, EUR, or SEK) at any time through your account settings. The new currency will apply to your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, all our plans include a free trial period:

  • Monthly plans include a 15-day free trial
  • 3-month plans include 1 month free
  • 6-month plans include 2 months free
  • Yearly plans include 3 months free

During the trial, you'll have full access to all features of your selected plan. No payment information is required to start your trial.

What kind of support is included?

All plans include technical support, but the level varies by plan:

  • Basic & Standard: Standard email support with 24-48 hour response time
  • Professional: Priority email support with 12-24 hour response time
  • Elite: Premium support with dedicated account manager, phone support, and 4-8 hour response time

Our Elite plan also includes personalized onboarding and training sessions to help you get the most out of your subscription.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes, we offer two ways to try our platform before purchasing:

  1. Free Demo: Access our demo environment instantly to explore features without setting up an account
  2. Free Trial: Register for a full-featured trial with your own account to test with your actual business data

You can access the demo by clicking the "Try Demo" button, or start your free trial by clicking "Register Now" on our pricing page.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

The Fooodis.com POS System is an all-in-one integrated platform that simplifies all aspects of running your restaurant. This includes order management, reservations, and improving customer service efficiency.
You can access the Fooodis.com POS system through your restaurant dashboard. Navigate to the POS section and click on 'Open POS' to launch the interface.
Within the POS interface, make sure to enable crucial preferences such as 'Enable POS,' 'Show External Orders,' and 'Order Finished Once Paid.' Remember to save your preferences to activate the POS system successfully.
The POS system allows you to manage orders through an intuitive interface. You can create new orders, modify existing ones, process payments, and track order status in real-time. The system also integrates with kitchen displays for seamless communication.
Personal orders are processed securely through our system. When a customer places an order, it's immediately sent to your dashboard and POS system. You can view, accept, and process these orders in real-time, ensuring efficient service and customer satisfaction.
Setting up pricing is simple through your restaurant dashboard. Navigate to the Menu section, where you can add, edit, or update prices for all your menu items. Changes are automatically synchronized with the POS system, ensuring consistent pricing across all platforms.
Key recommended features include the table management system, integrated payment processing, inventory tracking, sales reporting, customer database, and the kitchen display system. These features work together to streamline operations and enhance the customer experience.
For maximum efficiency, use a separate device dedicated to the POS system, apart from the device used to manage the restaurant dashboard. We recommend tablets (iPad or Android) for their portability and ease of use. For busy restaurants, consider having multiple devices to handle different sections or functions.
You can contact our support team through multiple channels: email at support@fooodis.com, live chat on our website, or by phone at the number provided in your account dashboard. Our support team is available 7 days a week to assist with any questions or issues you may encounter.
Visit our comprehensive knowledge base at help.fooodis.com for detailed guides, tutorials, and best practices. We also offer video tutorials on our YouTube channel and regular webinars for new users. Additionally, our blog contains valuable insights and tips for maximizing your restaurant's potential with our POS system.
