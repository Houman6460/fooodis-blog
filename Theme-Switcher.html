Customize Your Menu with Theme Switcher

Select the Perfect Look for Your Restaurant
Accessing the Theme Switcher:

Navigate to the Theme Switcher:
  ○ ​Log in to your Fooodis dashboard.
  ○ ​Go to the 'Apps' section and select 'Theme Switcher' from the options.

Choosing Your Theme:
  ○ ​You will see a dropdown menu labeled 'Select your menu template'. 
  ○ ​Click on it to see all available themes including Default, Elegant, Luxe, and Glow.
  ○ ​The 'Luxe template' is highly recommended for its sophisticated design and appealing visual elements, perfect for enhancing your online menu's appeal.

Applying the Theme:
  ○ ​Select your desired theme from the dropdown menu.
  ○ ​Click 'Save' to apply the theme to your online menu interface.

Keeping Your Menu Fresh:
  ○ ​Fooodis is committed to continually expanding the selection of themes and enhancing their functionalities. Stay tuned for new additions and updates which will be available based on your subscription plan.

FAQs: Navigating Theme Customization with Theme Switcher

Easily personalize your restaurant's online appearance with our Theme Switcher. Below are answers to some frequently asked questions to help you make the most of this feature.

What is the Theme Switcher in Fooodis?

The Theme Switcher allows you to change the visual design of your online menu, offering options like Luxe, Default, Elegant, and Glow to better align with your restaurant's branding.

How can I access the Theme Switcher?

Log into your Fooodis dashboard, click on 'Apps,' then select 'Theme Switcher' to view and select available menu themes.

What themes are currently available for my menu?

You can choose from four themes: Default, Elegant, Luxe (recommended for its superior design), and Glow.

Can I preview a theme before applying it to my menu?
  • –
  • –
    Yes, you can preview each theme in the Theme Switcher interface to see how it looks before making it live.
How often are new themes added to the Theme Switcher?

We regularly update our theme offerings based on current design trends and user feedback. Stay tuned for new additions and feature enhancements.

Is it possible to revert to a previous theme after trying a new one?

Yes, you can switch back to any previously used theme anytime by selecting it from the Theme Switcher menu.

Do all subscription plans have access to the Luxe theme?

Access to the Luxe theme and other premium themes depends on your subscription plan. Check your plan details for more information.
