### 1.2 Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and protect personal data when you (i) visit **fooodis.com** (the "Site"), (ii) use the Fooodis platform and mobile applications (the "Services"), or (iii) interact with us by email, phone or other channels.

### 1.3 Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

| Purpose                                    | Category of Personal Data                 | Legal Basis                                                               |

| Account creation & authentication          | Name, email, phone, company details       | Contract performance (Art. 6‑1‑b)                                         |

### 1.4 How We Share Data

* **Payment providers:** Swish, Klarna, Stripe receive payment‑related data necessary to execute transactions.
* **Hosting & Infrastructure:** AWS (EU‑Stockholm) for application hosting; Cloudflare for security/CDN.
* **Analytics & Support:** \[e.g. Google Analytics, Sentry, Intercom] – pseudonymised usage statistics and support tickets.

### 1.5 International Transfers

We store all production data within the European Economic Area (EEA). Should we transfer data outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and additional safeguards.

### 1.6 Data Retention

Personal data is retained for the shorter of (i) 10 years (Swedish accounting rules) or (ii) until deletion request, unless legal obligations require longer storage.

Under GDPR you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability or object to processing. Contact **[privacy@fooodis.com](mailto:privacy@fooodis.com)**.

### 1.8 Contact & Complaints

Questions or complaints? Email **[privacy@fooodis.com](mailto:privacy@fooodis.com)** or write to our DPO at the address above. You may also lodge a complaint with Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY) in Sweden.

## 1. Integritetspolicy

### 1.1 Vilka vi är

Logoland Design ("Logoland Design", "Fooodis", "vi", "oss") är en svensk enskild firma **ägd och drivs av Houman Ghavamzadeh**, organisationsnummer **197809195832**, med registrerad adress **Asperögatan 7b, 414 74 Göteborg, Sverige**. ### 1.3 Rättslig grund (GDPR art. 6)

| Ändamål                                     | Personuppgifter                                    | Rättslig grund                                                            |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
