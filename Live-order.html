Real-Time Order Management with Fooodis

Streamline Your Restaurant’s Order Workflow from Start to Finish

The Live Orders feature in Fooodis allows you to seamlessly manage orders in real-time, keeping your workflow organized and efficient. With easy-to-navigate categories like New Orders, Accepted Orders, and Done, you can track each stage of the order process with ease. The detailed view option enables you to access important customer information, payment status, and order details at any point. By managing orders from start to finish with just a few clicks, Fooodis helps you ensure smooth service and satisfied customers.

Step-by-Step Guide to Managing Live Orders

1. New Orders
  ○ All newly placed orders will appear in the New Orders section (3) on the dashboard. This area displays essential order information such as order number, customer name, and time of order.
  ○ You can click on the Details button (6) to get a full view of the order, including all items, customer information, and payment details. This is essential for confirming the order before proceeding.

2. Accepted Orders
  ○ Once you've reviewed the new order, click Accept (7) to move it into the Accepted Orders section (4). This signals to the kitchen or service staff to begin preparing the order.
  ○ All accepted orders remain here until they are marked as complete, giving you a clear view of what’s in progress.

3. Done Orders
  ○ After an order has been prepared and is ready for delivery or dine-in, mark it as Done (5). The order will then move to the Done section, indicating that it has been completed and fulfilled.
  ○ This helps keep the workflow organized by ensuring that no order remains in the Accepted phase after it's been served.

4. Viewing and Managing Order Details
  ○ At any point during the process, click Details (6) to view the full order summary. This includes important details like the VAT, customer info, dine method, and payment status, as shown in the first screenshot (7).
  ○ You can also print the order, email it, or view the status history to ensure every aspect of the order is tracked and managed properly.

FAQs for Live Order Management on Fooodis

The Live Orders feature in Fooodis is designed to help restaurants efficiently manage orders from the moment they are placed until they are fulfilled. Below are some frequently asked questions about how to use and maximize the Live Orders system, ensuring smooth operations and satisfied customers.

What is the Live Orders section?

The Live Orders section allows you to track and manage all incoming orders in real time. Orders are categorized into three stages: New, Accepted, and Done, making it easy to follow the progress of each order.

How do I view new orders?

New orders will appear in the New Orders column. You can see essential details like the order number and time placed. To view the full order details, click the Details button, which provides an overview of customer information, payment status, and order breakdown.

How do I accept an order?

To accept an order, simply click the Accept button next to the order in the New Orders column. The order will then move to the Accepted Orders column, signaling that the order is being prepared.

What happens after I accept an order?
  • –
  • –
    Once you accept an order, it moves to the Accepted Orders column, allowing your kitchen and service staff to begin preparing it. You can monitor its progress until it's ready to be marked as complete.
How do I mark an order as done?

After the order is fulfilled, you can click the Done button to move the order into the Done Orders column. This ensures the order is completed and no further action is needed.

Can I view detailed information about each order?

Yes, by clicking the Details button for any order, you can view a detailed summary including customer information, the items ordered, VAT, payment status, and more. This allows you to manage each order with complete transparency.

Can I print or email the order details?

Yes, within the detailed order view, you have the option to print the order or send it via email to your team or the customer directly for confirmation or record-keeping.

What is the purpose of the Status History?
  • –
    The Status History shows the timeline of the order, detailing when it was placed, accepted, and completed. This helps you track the progress of the order and ensures accurate record-keeping.
How do I handle rejected orders?
  • –
    If an order needs to be rejected, click the Reject button on the order details screen. The system will remove the order from the active queue, and you can notify the customer as necessary.
Can I track dine-in, takeout, and delivery orders in the Live Orders section?
  • –
    Yes, all types of orders—whether dine-in, takeout, or delivery—can be managed through the Live Orders section. Each order's method is listed in the details, ensuring that you and your staff can handle them appropriately.
