DNS Configuration
Configuring Your DNS Settings

Step-by-step instructions for seamless setup

After upgrading, you'll need to configure your DNS settings to point your domain to our servers. Log into your domain registrar’s website and create the following A records:

Host: * | Points to: 159.203.61.189 | TTL: 3600 seconds
Host: www | Points to: 159.203.61.189 | TTL: 600 seconds
Host: @ | Points to: 159.203.61.189 | TTL: 3600 seconds
These settings ensure that all variations of your domain name point to your Fooodis-powered website.

Verifying Your Custom Domain

Ensuring Successful Connection

Once you’ve set up your DNS records, go back to your Fooodis dashboard. Navigate to the "Custom Domain" section under the "Apps" menu and enter your custom domain name. Click "Verify Domain" to start the verification process. It typically takes 24 to 48 hours for the DNS changes to propagate and for your custom domain to become active, though it can sometimes be faster.

FAQ: Custom Domain Setup

Setting up a custom domain for your restaurant's online presence can enhance your brand's professionalism and credibility. Here are some frequently asked questions and their answers to guide you through the process of configuring your DNS settings and connecting your custom domain to our platform.

Do I need to upgrade to a custom domain immediately?

No, you can start using a free domain provided by Fooodis and upgrade to a custom domain when you are ready.

How long does it take for the custom domain to be active?

The domain verification process typically takes 24 to 48 hours but can sometimes be quicker.

What DNS records do I need to configure?

You need to create A records pointing your domain to our server's IP address (159.203.61.189), as detailed in our DNS configuration guide.

What if I encounter issues during the setup?
  • –
  • –
    If you face any difficulties, our support team is available to assist you with the setup process.
How do I configure my DNS settings to connect my custom domain?

To configure your DNS settings, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your domain registrar's account and navigate to the DNS settings section.
2. Add a new A record:
  ○ ​Hostname: *
  ○ ​Points to: 159.203.61.189
  ○ ​TTL: 3600 seconds
3. Add another A record:
  ○ ​Hostname: www
  ○ ​Points to: 159.203.61.189
  ○ ​TTL: 600 seconds
4. Add a final A record:
  ○ ​Hostname: @
  ○ ​Points to: 159.203.61.189
  ○ ​TTL: 3600 seconds
5. Save your DNS settings.
6. Return to the Fooodis dashboard, enter your custom domain in the provided field, and click "Verify Domain."

What happens if I don’t configure my custom domain?

If you don’t configure a custom domain, your restaurant’s online presence will be hosted under a Fooodis subdomain, such as https://fooodis.com/restaurant/perspolis/.

Can I start using my restaurant's site without a custom domain?

Yes, you can start using your restaurant’s site immediately with a Fooodis subdomain and switch to a custom domain later when you’re ready.
