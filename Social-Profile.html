Linking Social Media to Your Fooodis Restaurant Page

Boost Your Brand’s Online Interaction

Quickly enhance your restaurant's digital presence by integrating social media profiles directly into your Fooodis platform with this easy-to-follow guide.

Accessing Social Networks Settings

Navigate to Social Networks Settings:
   ○ From your Fooodis dashboard, click on the 'Social networks links' located under the 'Apps' section on the left-hand sidebar. This will take you to the page where you can manage your restaurant’s social media links.

Adding Social Media Profiles
Input Social Media URLs:
  ○ On the 'Social networks links' page, you will see fields for various social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, and Instagram. Enter the URLs of your restaurant’s profiles on these platforms in the corresponding fields.
Save Changes:
  ○ After entering all the necessary URLs, click the 'Save' button at the bottom of the page to update your restaurant’s profile with the social media links. This will ensure that these links are displayed on your restaurant’s page on the Fooodis platform, allowing customers to easily access your social media profiles.

Checking and Testing Links
Verify Links:
  ○ Once saved, it's crucial to verify that all links are correct and lead to the intended social media profiles. Incorrect links can frustrate customers and damage your restaurant's credibility.
Test Visibility and Functionality:
  ○ Check your restaurant’s public Fooodis page to see if the social media icons and links are displayed correctly and are functional. Click each link to ensure it redirects to the correct social media page.

FAQs: Integrating Social Media on Your Restaurant’s Fooodis Page

Explore essential FAQs to efficiently link and manage your restaurant’s social media profiles on the Fooodis platform. Whether you are new to digital marketing or looking to optimize your social presence, these answers will help you navigate the process effectively.

How do I add my restaurant’s Facebook page to the Fooodis platform?

Navigate to 'Social networks links' in your dashboard, find the Facebook field, and enter the URL of your restaurant’s Facebook page. Save changes to update.

Can I link multiple social media profiles to my Fooodis restaurant page?

Yes, you can link multiple social media accounts including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube by entering the URLs in the respective fields on the 'Social networks links' page.

What should I do if a social media link is not working?

Double-check the URL for any errors and ensure it is correctly formatted. If the problem persists, verify that there are no restrictions on the linked social media profile that might prevent access.
